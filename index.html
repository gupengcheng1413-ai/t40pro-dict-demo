The Power of Small Changes。Many people believe that they need to do something extraordinary to change the world. However, the truth is that small actions can make a big difference. For example, reducing the use of plastic bags or turning off the lights when leaving a room may seem like small things, but they are essential for protecting our environment.

In our daily lives, we can also help others with simple acts of kindness. A warm smile or a helpful hand can brighten someone's day. As the saying goes, "Great things are done by a series of small things brought together." If everyone contributes a little effort, our world will become a much better place to live in.

art.(用于单数可数名词前，表示未曾提到的) 一(人事、物);一(个，件...)
考点解析

【搭配】 have a good time玩得开心 / have a picnic去野餐 / a university 一所大学 / a useful thing 一样有用的东西

【语法点】 a, an和the都是冠词，a和an是不定冠词，the是定冠词：

·以元音音素开头的单数可数名词前用不定冠词an

·以辅音音素开头的单数可数名词前用不定冠词a

·the常见用法: 特指或上文已提到过的人或物。用于宇宙中独一无二的事物的名词前。用于最高级和序数词前。

考题解析

①——Did you get there by ____ bike? ——No, I took ____ taxi.( )

A. a;a B./;a C.the; the D. a; the

解析: 根据句意可知是考查搭配"坐出租车"，用take a taxi。故选:B。
